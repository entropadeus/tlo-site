About Me

Why This Work Matters to Me

Estate planning isn't just legal documents—it's about protecting the people you love most.

I'm Stephen Turkett, a Fort Worth native who's spent over 25 years helping Texas families navigate life's most important decisions. What sets my practice apart isn't just legal expertise—it's personal understanding.

I spend time with my clients to know and understand their goals and needs. I do my best to communicate using regular words and cut through the legalese.

I have personal experience caring for elderly family members, family members with special needs, business deals, and, unfortunately, even divorce. I can't always make your problems go away, but I pledge to guide you through them with compassion and integrity.

Beyond the Law Office

When I'm not helping families secure their futures, you'll find me playing guitar at my church, spending time with my wife and adult children, or getting lost in a good historical mystery novel. Family isn't just what I do—it's who I am.
