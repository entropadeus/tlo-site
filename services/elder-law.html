Medicaid Planning

Navigate Medicaid Qualification

Medicaid planning is one of the most complex areas of elder law, with strict asset and income limits that can disqualify families from benefits.

We help Texas families navigate these regulations while preserving their life savings through strategic planning techniques like asset protection trusts, spend-down strategies, and proper timing of transfers.

  • Asset protection strategies
  • Spend-down planning
  • Medicaid application assistance
  • Appeals and denials
  • Crisis Medicaid planning
Long-Term Care

Plan for Quality Care

Long-term care costs can quickly deplete a family's savings. In Texas, nursing home costs average $5,000-7,000 per month.

We work with families to develop comprehensive long-term care plans that protect assets, ensure quality care, and preserve inheritance for the next generation. This includes evaluating insurance options, creating care directives, and establishing legal frameworks for care decisions.

  • Long-term care insurance analysis
  • Care facility evaluation
  • Asset preservation strategies
  • Family caregiver agreements
  • Veterans benefits planning
Asset Protection

Preserve Your Family's Wealth

Protecting your family's assets from long-term care costs requires careful planning and knowledge of Texas and federal laws.

We help families implement legal strategies to preserve wealth while maintaining eligibility for government benefits. From irrevocable trusts to strategic gifting, there are numerous tools available. The key is implementing these strategies early, before care is needed.

  • Irrevocable asset protection trusts
  • Strategic gifting programs
  • Homestead protection planning
  • Business succession planning
  • Annuity and life insurance strategies
Veterans Benefits

Aid & Attendance Benefits

Many Texas veterans and their spouses are unaware of the long-term care benefits available through the VA.

The Aid & Attendance benefit can provide up to $2,431 per month for veterans or $1,318 for surviving spouses to help pay for care. We help veteran families navigate the complex application process, gather required documentation, and structure assets to qualify for these valuable benefits.

  • Aid & Attendance applications
  • Asset restructuring for VA benefits
  • Medical evidence gathering
  • Appeals and denials
  • Coordination with other benefits
